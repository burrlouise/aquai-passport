How it works
1

Breeder creates the passport

When a fish is ready to sell, the breeder logs in, fills in the species, health, water parameters and their verified identity. A unique passport ID is generated instantly.

2

Passport travels with the fish

A QR code is attached to the bag, box, or listing. Every handler in the chain can scan it to verify the fish's identity and update the custody record.

3

Buyer scans and claims

The new owner scans the QR, claims the fish, and instantly receives a personalised acclimation guide based on the origin water parameters. The chain of custody is complete.
